# Sub-recipe: Wyoming-LLC

For mail from the Wyoming Secretary of State or the LLC's registered agent —
entity-maintenance items, not tax-return items (US federal/IRS matters route
to `us-irs-notice.md` instead, even though the LLC is the same entity).

## How to read it

- Confirm it's from/about the Wyoming Secretary of State (or forwarded by
  the registered agent on the SOS's behalf) rather than the IRS.
- Identify the document type: Annual Report reminder, Annual Report
  delinquency notice, Notice of Pending Administrative Dissolution, or
  Service of Process (a lawsuit/legal notice being forwarded).

## Common document types

| Document | What it means | Urgency |
|---|---|---|
| Annual Report reminder | Routine — due by the first day of the LLC's anniversary month of formation | 🟢 Low if well ahead of the date, 🟡 Moderate if within a few weeks |
| Annual Report delinquency notice | Report and/or license tax not yet filed/paid past due date | 🟡 Moderate — grace period before dissolution |
| Notice of Pending Administrative Dissolution | LLC will be dissolved by the state if not cured | 🔴 Critical — hard deadline, loss of good standing / entity |
| Service of Process forwarded by registered agent | The LLC is party to a legal action | 🔴 Critical — response deadlines are set by the court, not the SOS, and are usually short |
| Registered agent renewal/change notice | Administrative, agent continuity | 🟢 Low unless it implies a lapse in agent coverage |

## Next action patterns

- **Annual Report (routine or delinquent)**: "file the Annual Report and pay
  the license tax via the Wyoming SOS online filing portal before the due
  date."
- **Pending dissolution**: "file the delinquent Annual Report and pay all
  outstanding license tax immediately — the state will administratively
  dissolve the LLC after the stated cure period."
- **Service of process**: "read the attached legal document in full and get
  it in front of counsel immediately — do not treat this as routine mail."

## Documents/info typically needed

- Wyoming filing ID / SOS entity number.
- The LLC's total assets located in Wyoming (drives the minimum license tax
  calculation, at least $60).
- Registered agent name and contact.
- For service of process: the underlying legal document itself, forwarded
  in full — never just the registered agent's cover sheet.
